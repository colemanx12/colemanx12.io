Welcome to Garnet Love Foundation.Garnet Love Foundation, A non governmental organization of youths who have a goal which is reaching out to children and youths like them who have have been deprived of the necessities of life. This foundation has visited orphanages, streets, communities etc. Giving out food, gifts, items and other things to enhance the living of these ones. The charity has experienced growth and is still growing as it aims at reaching out to a whole lot of children and youths. This charity visions doing great things within their reach for these children and youths. They deserve to live better You can always join us on this train and make a difference. Thank you.
