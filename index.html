About GLF

Hey! Welcome to Garnet Love Foundation.Garnet Love Foundation, A non governmental organization of youths who have a goal which is reaching out to youths and children like them who have have been deprived of the necessities of life which they should have been able to enjoy. The charity organization began officially on the 31st of December 2017 by Ajayi Oluwafumbi who is the president of this organization. There are circumstances surrounding the existence of this charity organization. It came about as a result of some happening at that time and Fumbi was unable you get the help she needed, she did not exactly go through what those children go through but she experienced deprivation. As a result of this, she called some of her friends together and they thought it was a good idea. Together they started small, they started reaching out to children and youths through different means. This charity has visited orphanages, streets, communities etc. Giving out food, gifts, items and other things to enhance the living of these ones. The charity has experienced growth and is still growing as it aims at reaching out to a whole lot of children and youths. This charity visions doing great things within their reach for these children and youths. They deserve to live better You can always join us on this train and make a difference.
